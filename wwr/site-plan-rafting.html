Overview

Purpose

At Rafting Travel, our mission is to ignite the spark of adventure in everyone, transforming them into confident and skilled rafters. We are dedicated to the thrill of rafting by providing accessible training programs and low cost gear rental. Whether you're a novice seeking the excitement of your first river journey or an experienced rafter craving more challenging waters. Join us in exploring the untamed beauty of rivers, mastering the art of rafting, and forging connections with a community of like-minded enthusiasts.

Audience

What do they want? To escape to a new adventure away from home.

What are their needs that aren't being met? We have the gear, the training and the expertise take you safely down rivers of your individual skill level

How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? Information is online and available at your time.
